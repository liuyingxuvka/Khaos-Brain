## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: SkillGuard is author-side maintenance supervision
The maintainer repository MAY keep one current SkillGuard contract source,
compiled contract, and check manifest inside each maintained skill's source
tree. SkillGuard SHALL use those files only to verify the skill's own declared
promise, checks, evidence subjects, and depth criteria before distribution. It
MUST NOT invent a target-domain obligation, deepen a target criterion, choose a
consumer route, execute consumer work, or become installed authority.

The maintainer repository SHALL carry one current private author-repository
manifest and marker-bounded author prompt projection for the explicitly listed
maintenance units. Author execution evidence SHALL remain unit-local and
immutable. Evidence lifecycle audit and garbage-collection planning MUST be
snapshot-bound and read-only; quarantine and purge require separate explicit
authority and MUST NOT treat directory membership or age alone as proof that
an object is unreachable.

#### Scenario: Current author adoption is refreshed
- **WHEN** the installed SkillGuard author format is newer than this repository's author control surface
- **THEN** adoption replaces the old author projection directly for the same explicit units and MUST NOT scan unrelated consumer skills into maintenance membership

#### Scenario: Compiler-owned outputs are stale
- **WHEN** an unchanged target source contract is compiled by the current SkillGuard compiler
- **THEN** only its compiled contract and check manifest are regenerated, while its declared obligations, routes, checks, owners, evidence subjects, and depth criteria remain target-owned and unchanged

#### Scenario: Author evidence lifecycle is inspected
- **WHEN** the maintainer audits an isolated owner-evidence root and produces a garbage-collection plan
- **THEN** both operations are read-only and snapshot-bound, and no evidence object is quarantined or purged without a separately authorized exact current plan

#### Scenario: Native business execution is outside a source-only refresh
- **WHEN** author adoption and generated-contract parity can be verified without a current native maintenance receipt
- **THEN** source-only assurance MAY pass its bounded author-control claim but MUST NOT claim that Sleep, Dream, organization work, or manual software update executed or completed

#### Scenario: A maintained skill is installed on another computer
- **WHEN** the installer projects a maintained source skill into a Codex home
- **THEN** the installed tree contains no author manifest, `.skillguard` directory, SkillGuard command, author receipt, router state, or evidence-lifecycle dependency
